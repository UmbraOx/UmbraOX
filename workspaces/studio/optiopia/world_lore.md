# Optiopia — World Lore

## Overview
In Optiopia, three gods created a world filled with resources and demigods/demihumans to explore new flavors. Humans were made to farm and worship these gods. The player wakes up discovering free will and embarks on quests to find the best ingredients for divine dishes.

## Creation
The three gods created the world while cooking a dish. An ingredient mix-up resulted in an unparalleled meal, leading to the creation of demigods/demihumans and humans who farm resources.

## Factions

### Harvesters Guild
A group of humans dedicated to farming and gathering.

### Shadowspike Cult
A secretive faction seeking forbidden knowledge.

## Gods & Powers

### Aetheria — Healing and Nature
The god of rejuvenation, associated with the mystic grove.

### Terrestrialus — Earth and Strength
The god of stability, linked to crystal tower.

### Pyroflux — Fire and Cooking
The god of culinary arts, connected to aurora fields.

## Towns

### Greenhaven
A peaceful farming community.

### Aurorastead
A bustling town with a focus on culinary arts.
